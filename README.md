# SACCO Credit Risk Concentration Analysis
## Objective
Identify risk concentration and early-warning signals in a SACCO loan portfolio using simulated data.
## Key Insights
- Portfolio risk is highly concentrated in large loan exposures.
- Stress testing reveals non-linear risk acceleration.
- A small minority of loans drive the majority of portfolio vulnerability.
## Methods
- Risk-weighted behavioral analysis
- Pareto concentration analysis
- Stress testing under interest and behavioral shocks
## Outputs
- Executive memos
- Excel-based risk models
- Visual risk concentration evidence
## Tools
Excel, Risk Weighting, Portfolio Stress Testing
